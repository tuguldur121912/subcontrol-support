Common Questions

Does SubControl connect to my bank?

No. SubControl does not connect to banks or financial institutions.

Where is my data stored?

Your subscription data is stored locally on your device.

Can I export my data?

Yes. SubControl supports CSV export and encrypted backup/restore.

How do I restore purchases?

Open SubControl → Settings → Restore Purchases.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete subscriptions inside the app or remove the app from your device.
