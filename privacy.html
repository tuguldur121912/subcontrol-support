SubControl Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

SubControl is a privacy-first subscription tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user data.

Data Collection

SubControl does not collect, sell, or share personal data. The app does not require an account, does not connect to banks, and does not access financial institutions.

Local Data Storage

Subscription information entered by the user is stored locally on the user’s device. This may include subscription names, prices, currencies, categories, billing cycles, renewal dates, and reminder preferences.

Notifications

SubControl may use local notifications for renewal reminders and monthly recap reminders if the user enables them. These notifications are generated locally on the device.

CSV Export and Backup

Users may export subscription data as CSV or create encrypted backup files. These actions are user-initiated. SubControl does not upload these files to a server.

In-App Purchases

SubControl may offer in-app purchases. Payments are processed by Apple or Google through their respective app stores. SubControl does not collect payment card information.

Third-Party Services

SubControl does not use bank-sync services, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or cloud tracking services.

Data Deletion

Users can delete subscriptions inside the app. Users can also delete all local app data by removing the app from their device.
